Frequently Asked Questions

Is video compression free?

Yes, our video compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges.

Will compression reduce video quality?

Some quality reduction is inevitable when compressing, but our tool optimizes the balance between file size and quality.

What's the maximum video size I can compress?

You can compress videos up to 2GB in size. For larger files, consider splitting them first.

Are my videos stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens in your browser. Your videos never leave your device.
